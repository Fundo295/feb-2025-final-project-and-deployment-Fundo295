The World's Oceans

Oceans cover 71% of Earth's surface and contain 97% of the planet's water.

Ocean Zones

Oceans are divided into zones based on depth and light penetration: the sunlight zone, twilight zone, midnight zone, abyss, and trenches.

Marine Biodiversity

Oceans are home to an incredible variety of life, from microscopic plankton to the largest animal on Earth, the blue whale.

Ocean Conservation
